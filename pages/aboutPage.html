About Us

What Do We Stand For?

At OnlyPhones, we stand for two key principles: saving money and saving the environment. We believe in providing affordable options for purchasing and selling phones while simultaneously contributing to a sustainable future by extending the lifespan of electronic devices and reducing e-waste.

Saving Money and the Environment

Buying a brand new phone can often feel like a significant investment, with the latest models commanding high prices. However, opting for a quality, gently used device from OnlyPhones allows you to enjoy all the features you need at a fraction of the cost. By choosing pre-owned phones, you can potentially save hundreds of dollars compared to purchasing new.

The environmental impact of electronic waste, or e-waste, is a growing concern worldwide. Did you know that over 80% of phones generally end up in landfills? This contributes to pollution, resource depletion, and various environmental hazards. By extending the lifespan of phones through reuse and recycling, we can significantly reduce e-waste and its harmful effects on the planet.
